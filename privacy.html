Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Infinyte processes, why we use it, who we share it with, and which controls you have over your information.

01

Controller and scope

  • Infinyte operates this service for mobile devices.
  • This policy covers use of the app and related services for authentication, profile, subscriptions, and notifications.
02

Data we process

  • Account data: email, password (managed by Supabase Auth), first and last name.
  • Optional profile data: avatar/photo, phone number, date of birth, occupation, and location that you choose to provide.
  • Financial data you enter in the app: accounts, transactions, budgets, debts, goals, and planner entries.
  • Subscription data: plan status and purchase events validated through Apple/Google stores and RevenueCat.
  • Access policy data tied to your plan, including Free-tier limits (currently up to 3 active budgets and 3 active goals).
  • Minimum technical data: app version, operating system, and error events required for operation and security.
03

Where your data is stored

  • Your financial records are primarily stored on your device in an encrypted Realm database at rest.
  • Supabase infrastructure stores authentication data, basic profile data, preferences, and avatar (if uploaded).
  • Backups enabled from the app are generated locally on your device.
04

Device permissions

  • Camera and photo library: only to take or choose a profile photo.
  • Biometrics: only for local app unlock; we do not receive your fingerprint or Face ID template.
  • Notifications: only if you enable reminders or background tasks.
  • We currently do not use contacts, location, calendar, or microphone for production app features.
05

How we use data

  • Create and protect your account, authenticate sessions, and prevent unauthorized use.
  • Display and process your finances inside the app.
  • Sync specific cloud elements: profile, avatar, preferences, and in-app notifications.
  • Manage subscriptions, restores, and premium feature access.
  • Comply with legal obligations and valid authority requests.
06

Third parties with limited access

  • Supabase: authentication, database, and avatar storage.
  • Google: only if you choose Google Sign-In.
  • RevenueCat and Apple/Google stores: purchase management and subscription status.
  • We do not sell your data or share it for third-party advertising.
07

Retention and deletion

  • We keep account and profile data while your account is active or as legally required.
  • You can remove your avatar from within the app at any time.
  • Local data can be removed by uninstalling the app or clearing/restoring device storage.
  • To request deletion of account/server-side data, use the support channels available in the app.
08

Your rights

  • Request access, correction, updates, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw optional consents (for example biometrics or notifications) from settings.
  • Depending on your country, you may also have portability or objection rights.
09

Security and international transfers

  • We apply encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), local encryption at rest, and access controls.
  • Because we use global providers, some processing may occur outside your country with contractual safeguards.
10

Changes to this policy

  • We may update this policy to reflect technical or legal changes.
  • If a change is material, we will notify you inside the app before it takes effect.

If you do not agree with this policy, you must stop using the app. Continued use means you accept the current version.
